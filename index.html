Welcome to DeTox

Trying to detoxify the internet, one gamer at a time

What are we?

A site aimed at providing resources for marginalized groups and their allies in the gaming community

Our Goals

To provide a way for queer gamers to speak up and share their experiences.
To show new gamers what developers are doing to support their community.
And laslty, to educate gamers and developers about what they can do to help.
