List of Common Offensive Words/Actions

This is a semi-comprehensive list of words used to alienate minority groups or actions that demonstrate bias and antipathy towards these groups. Use this list, along with other factors, to determine if someone is genuinely harassing someone else during a game you're playing.

In many cases, these words have slipped into the common vernacular without much memory about where they came from or how they're offensive. The people that remember are those most affected by it.

Limp-Wristed Gay men, especially feminine ones. It is based on a stereotype that effeminate gay men hold their wrists in a limp or "weak" position."" The term implies that being gay is the same as being feminine. It also casts aspersions on the idea of men being feminine.
